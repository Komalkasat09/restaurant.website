About Us

Welcome to SULAX restaurant At Sulax restaurant, we believe that dining out is not just about eating—it’s about creating memories, celebrating milestones, and savoring flavors that tantalize the taste buds. Nestled in the heart of Jalgaon, our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a culinary experience that leaves a lasting impression.



Our Story:-
Founded in 2005, Sulax restaurant has been a labor of love from the very beginning. It all started with a vision to redefine traditional dining by infusing it with innovation, passion, and a commitment to excellence. What began as a humble eatery has now blossomed into a culinary destination renowned for its delectable dishes and warm hospitality.



Our Philosophy:-
At Sulax restaurant, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Every dish on our menu is crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local farmers and artisans. From farm to table, we strive to create a dining experience that not only delights the palate but also nourishes the soul.



Our Team:-
Behind every exceptional meal is a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about their craft. Our chefs are culinary artisans, each bringing their unique expertise and creativity to the kitchen. From our front-of-house staff to our talented culinary team, every member of the Sulax restaurant family is committed to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience.



Community:-
As a locally-owned and operated restaurant, we take pride in being a part of the vibrant community of Jalgaon. We believe in giving back to the community that has given us so much, which is why we actively support local charities, organizations, and events.
